A Celebration of Life

Christine Coates

Sunrise: January 27th, 1947

Sunset: October 17th, 2022

Christine is now an angel in Heaven, watching over those who love and miss her dearly.

Christine Coates was born to Robert Lee Coats and Tenia Mae Coats. She was a lovely sister to Minnie, Robert JR., Florence, Ellis, and Harrison.

Christine leaves behind a beautiful daughter named Brandy, who, along with her blessed grandchildren, will continue her legacy.

A brief timeline of just some of the notable points of Christine's life..

  • 1947 — Born into the world in Marion, Alabama
  • 1965 — Graduated from Boteler High School
  • 1970 — Graduated from Adams State College, in Colorado

Other notable events with unknown dates..

  • Was a dedicated church goer at Morning Star Baptist Church
  • Became a high school teacher in the Chicago School System
  • Served others in the Departmen of Human Resources in Tuscaloosa, AL

"When we are born, we are a light unto this world.. And when we expire, our light flourishes in all that we have touched, illuminating the world we leave behind. The light that Christine has left behind shines on in all who knew her, all who loved her, and all she touched."

-- Brandy Nicole Coates Williams
